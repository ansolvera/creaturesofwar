The Developers

Tim Brunetto

Founder of Chaos Entertainment. His love for video games began with the Megaman series and heavily evolved over the years. Loves old school games, super ghouls and ghosts is one of his favorites.

Jay Suong

Likes coding tools by day, and enjoys getting into shenanigans with friends in games by night.

Andrew De Lange

Andrew is a classically trained musician who’s spent the last 15 years composing and producing music. His compositions are always inspired by some kind of imaginative space, which is why he’s passionate about writing for video games. His style is smooth and cerebral, but combines many types of instruments, rhythms and themes.

Ashleii Kono

Ashleii Kono is a digital artist with a demonstrated history of working in the computer games industry. In her free time, she enjoys sketching, gaming, and visiting the local rock climbing gyms.

Gregory Mcmanus

Having started gaming with World of Warcraft and most recently playing Resident Evil 8, Greg has a wide range of tastes in stories. He likes to tell his own stories where he can and prefers to do so via the realm of audio. He started with music but found his true calling through sound design and implementation within video games.
